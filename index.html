Cindy Van Dyne

New album "3 seconds before midnight"

Cindy Van Dyne is a 24-year-old pop music sensation with a unique blend of Australian and Thai heritage. Known for her captivating voice and vibrant stage presence, Cindy first captured the spotlight with her hit single "Later" in 2019, which became a global favorite and established her as a rising star in the music industry. Her artistry combines heartfelt lyrics with infectious beats, creating a sound that resonates across cultures. In 2024, Cindy released her much-anticipated album, 3 Seconds Before Midnight, showcasing her growth as an artist and her ability to craft deeply emotional and thought-provoking tracks. With a devoted fan base and a talent for pushing creative boundaries, Cindy Van Dyne continues to make waves as one of pop music's brightest stars.
